<h4>Networking Terms</h4>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Sybil Attack Resistance:</strong> A system's defense against fake identities trying to control the network.</li>
    <li><strong>Incentivized Infrastructure:</strong> Giving rewards for contributing resources or tasks in a network.</li>
    <li><strong>Native UDP Routing:</strong> Direct routing of data using UDP packets for low-latency communication.</li>
    <li><strong>Native Decentralized Name System:</strong> A decentralized system for managing names without a central authority.</li>
    <li><strong>Router Consensus:</strong> Agreement among routers on network configurations and decisions.</li>
</ul>
